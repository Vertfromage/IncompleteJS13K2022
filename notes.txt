- To put out fire:
1. hit it with water
2. burns out and leaves yellowed grass after time elapses

- To restore color:
1. hit it with water when it is yellowed
2. changes to puddle
3. after time elapses turn to restored color

To damage player: 
1. hit with water

To restore fire:
1. Go back to flaming grass

To lose:
1. Run out of fire

To win level:
1.burn down all the trees


Game Progression:
1. Levels randomly generated
2. More water elves each turn
